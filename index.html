OUR STORY

In 2015, Ivan and Arvin Milan opened Milan Café in Bulan, Sorsogon, blending their skills in coffee brewing and baking. Housed in an old family home, their cozy café quickly became a local favorite, known for its warm atmosphere, delicious pastries, and community events.
